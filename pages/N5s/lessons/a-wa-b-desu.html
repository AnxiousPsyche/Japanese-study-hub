Basic Sentence Pattern

PATTERN 1

A は B です

Basic Sentence Pattern

A

↓

Subject

Person / Thing
we are talking about

は

↓

Topic Marker

Indicates the topic.
Similar to "is / am / are" but not exactly.

B

↓

Predicate

Information
about the subject.

です

↓

Polite Ending

Makes the sentence
polite.

EXAMPLES

私はリアです。

Watashi wa Ria desu.

I am Ria.

これは本です。

Kore wa hon desu.

This is a book.

田中さんは先生です。

Tanaka-san wa sensei desu.

Mr. Tanaka is a teacher.

🔄 More Examples

The topic (A) in A は B です doesn't always have to be わたし (I). You can replace it with other people, objects, or things.

彼は先生です。

Kare wa sensei desu.

He is a teacher.

これは本です。

Kore wa hon desu.

This is a book.

それは車です。

Sore wa kuruma desu.

That is a car.

あれは学校です。

Are wa gakkou desu.

That (over there) is a school.

💡 Remember

In the pattern A は B です, the A can be many different things.

  • わたし — I / Me
  • かれ — He
  • かのじょ — She
  • これ — This
  • それ — That (near the listener)
  • あれ — That (far from both people)
